Introducing ProjectConnect
We want to help you help others!

Ever have an idea that you knew could make a difference in the lives of others? Have you ever discarded this idea in anticipation of all the work you simply couldn't finish by yourself? We want you to know that any idea that can make a difference is worth acting upon. ProjectConnect will help connect you with people who can help you accomplish your task or help those who want to contribute the world get connected to a project that motivates them. Fight apathy and change the world!

How?

By taking a simple survey about your interests, ProjectConnect will direct you to a chat group filled with people who share the same passion and who you work together with to make a difference NOW.

Have An Idea Already?

Simply upload your idea to the website with your email and contact information.

Accessibility

ProjectConnect will connect you with people living in your region so that meeting up and coming together to initiate your project will no longer be a problem.
